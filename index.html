When I finished my Bachelor's Degree in Secondary Education major in General Sciences from MSU-Iligan Institute of Technology (IIT), Iligan City in 2019, I decided to stay at home and relax for awhile before looking for jobs or preparing for the Board Exam. But a close-friend of mine who is working at Mindanao Mission Academy decided to leave her teaching work and ask me to replace her. Without any second thought, I accept the offer and went on to start my job as a teacher. And there I meet my man, then, several months later, OUR story begins
