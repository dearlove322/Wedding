Our WEDDINGof FAITH

The BRIDE

Reda Babia Quidet
The story on how I meet the man God had prepared for me was truly amazing. When I finished my Bachelor's Degree in Secondary Education major in General Sciences from MSU-Iligan Institute of Technology (IIT), Iligan City in 2019, I decided to stay at home and relax for awhile before looking for jobs or preparing for the Board Exam. But a close-friend of mine who is working at Mindanao Mission Academy decided to leave her teaching work and ask me to replace her. Without any second thought, I accept the offer and went on to start my job as a teacher. And there I meet my man, then, several months later, OUR story begins

The GROOM

Bernabe Josol Apare
I graduated with the degree of Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Biological Sciences from Mountain View College, Bukidnon. After my graduation in 2018, I was accepted to work as Science Teacher at Mindanao Mission Academy. I was supposed to go outside the country but God didn't allow it because before my flight, COVID-19 striked and there was a national lockdown, yet I Praise God for that because during that COVID lockdown I meet the woman God had prepared for me.

Our Story

September 2019
Our First Meet

Substitute Teacher. September 17, 2019 when the bride arrived Mindanao Mission Academy as a substitute teacher. Never in her wildest dream the plan of connecting to this school. However, one of her friend from IIT, Ms. Charina Genosolango, a current science teacher in MMA that time decided to move out to DSWD Caraga. As a fresh graduate, she was asked to sub the workload of Ms. Charina. Few days after her arrival, the school held its CeSA Days, it was then when the bride and the groom get acquainted to each other because the belong to the same group for the Faculty Presentation.

October 2019
Start sa Sungog2x

Yakimix. First semester of the SY:2019-2020 has ended and the MMA Admin held a thank giving treat at Yakimix, Cagayan de Oro City (at that time, classes starts in June and ends in March). When it comes to pairing of single teachers, our Principal Pastor Jesreel D. Mercader has that kind of talent. And with that, the two, together with other single teachers has no way to escape the pairing and teasing from their fellow teachers.

March 2020
Start of intimate closeness (Pandemic time)

Involvement in the Ministry and the Best thing during Pandemic. Whenever there is an opportunity for the ministry outside the campus, Ma'am Marchie Mercader (the wife of the Principal), will request both the bride and the groom to join. With all those pairing and teasing, the two doesn't have the plan to take it seriously because the groom has decided to venture abroad and is waiting for his flight scheduled May 7, 2020 bound for Thailand. However, March 13, 2020 when the Government declared for a National Lockdown due to COVID-19 and all travels are cancelled. It was a horrible pandemic, and the groom realized that his planned of venturing to Thailand is no longer possible. Though that pandemic was a horrible experience to recall, but it was also the time when the bride and the groom decided to mingle together inside the very peaceful campus of Mindanao Mission Academy. Read more on "God's Perfect Timing" in the story below.

June 2020
Announcement of our Relationship

The 3 Months Rule. While the saying that says "long observation, short engagement" is still the best, our Principal had also another philosophy; and that is the 3 months rule. Three months observation, and if both individuals are compatible to each other, no need to wait for a very long time before engaging into a relationship because while inside the relationship, the observation for both still continues. Since the bride and the groom notices that they understand each other and have mutual feelings and ideas, they decided to follow the 3 months rule.

August 2021
Engagement

Online Engagement. Fourteen months after being in a relationship, on August 22, 2021, the two decided to settle for good. No more expensive proposals. There is no bouquet or any flower or any lights that resembles a heart shape, nothing. Instead of a ring, the groom bought a cheap couple watch from Shopee to signify a lifetime commitment together. After a moment of discussion and prayer, the two decided to get engage, same time started wearing the couple watch. During the proposal, no other people around, but the couple believe that Angels are watching and they are the witnesses of the lifetime commitment that the lovers had made. August 29, was the schedule when the groom would formally aske the bride's parents for the approval of their plan. However, an unexpected situation happened four days before the scheduled date that forced them to do the Engagement via online using the Zoom Platform. See "The Online Engagement" in the story below.

December 2021
Wedding

The Wedding of Faith. Isaiah 60:22 reminds that when the time is right, God will make it happened. Having a wedding is not easy. It takes thousands of money and so much preparation, but the lovers doesn't have any. A very little money, always busy in the workplace, but still it never hinders that the wedding will succeed. It's not because they have the right time, but God made the time right for them. Read full story: "Our Wedding of Faith" below.
