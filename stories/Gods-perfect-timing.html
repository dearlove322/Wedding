God's Perfect Timing

Bernabe J. Apare

      In my Diary dated May 20, 2014; I put into writing all the characteristics that I look for from a prospective life partner.
While I cannot deny the fact that I also admired several ladies during my adolescent years, I always put in mind that I should set a standard as I look for a prospective life partner. These standards focus on the four major areas: Physical Characteristics, Moral Aspects, Religiosity, and I added more on the Miscellaneous.
These includes:

Physical Characteristics

  1. Slightly chubby
  2. Oblong ang face
  3. Straight hair
  4. At least 5 ft tall but not more than 5’3
  5. Fair skin color
  6. Of course beautiful (ikasuroy)
  7. Not fun óf using make-ups, lipstick, eye liner, etc.
Moral Aspect
  1. Honest
  2. Industrious
  3. Loving
  4. Caring
  5. Not flirting
  6. Not an easy girl
  7. Thrift
  8. Intelligent
  9. Helpful
  10. Patient
  11. Not arrogant
  12. Humble
  13. Diligent
  14. Unselfish
  15. Self-confident
  16. Hospitable
  17. Friendly
  18. Faithful
Religiosity
  1. Same faith (Adventist)
  2. She relay mostly to God
  3. Willingness to learn God’s word
  4. Active in participating any religious services
  5. Have fun of going to church and any religious services
  6. Confident in sharing to others her knowledge about God
  7. Willingness to serve others
  8. Have the desire to bring others to Christ
Miscellaneous
  1. Level of affection
  2. A working student
  3. dili pa kaayu daghan ug uyab nga naagian
  4. Virgin
  5. Willing to understand my attitude
  6. Have principles in keeping her own
  7. Appreciate my achievements
  8. Willing to support me in my dreams and ambitions
  9. Related courses (Biological Sciences or any science related courses)
  10. Acquaintance with the family
  11. Willing to accept of who I am
  12. Able to show that she needs me and is willing to be with me.
  13. Have skills in: music, art, literary, livelihood, etc.
  14. Name should begin one of this letters: G, J, R, Z
During this time, I started praying to God that He will give the lady that I possess if not all but at least 95% of the characteristics I am looking for. I also prayed that I will get married when I reach 27 years old.
Time passed by, whenever I admire a lady; I secretly put a rating using my standard as the criteria. The moment I finished college, I also found my sweetheart whom I think, she’s already the one God had prepared for me. However, after three years of being in a relationship, we noticed that our relationship is no longer healthy, so we decided to part ways.


Thailand Plan
After 1 year of working here in MMA, still, I can never see myself working here forever. I am not satisfied with where God put me so I decided to make my own decision to explore the world and Thailand is my option. It was the year 2019 when I fixed my decision to leave MMA to follow my dreams to explore the other side of the world.

Here Comes A Proxy
   School Year 2019-2020 started and it’s my second year working here in MMA and supposed to be my last year since I will move to Thailand after the school year ends. As usual, when new school started, new teachers join the MMA’s teaching force, and one of those was Ms. Charina Genosolango, a science teacher and a graduate from MSU-IIT.
      Things went all well until the month of September before the 1st semester ended when Ms. Charina decided to leave MMA to pursue her new work in the Government, particularly in DSWD Caraga. The administration supported her desire to move-out but also asked her if she can recommend someone who can replace her since it was an eleventh-hour decision and the administration doesn’t have any time to look for and hire a new teacher. Ms. Charina then called her friend, a fellow IITians as well as fellow MAS AMICUS of Iligan City who graduated a few months ago and at that time was already spending the comfort of her home in Gingoog City. Since Charina needed to leave the following day, the MMA Principal, it was Dr. Albert Ponsica that time while Ptr. Mercader was serving in the district, and requested her friend Reda Quidet to report immediately to take the place of Charina in her teaching load as well as her Advisory, the Grade 11-Zircon Section that time.
      September 17, 2019 when Reda arrived here in MMA. Same as the usual day, I attended the Faculty Morning Devotional and I saw her in front of the Admin Building. Since I am the type of person who, most of the time, doesn’t want to talk if not being asked, I just passed by since I also don't have the idea that she was the “proxy”of Charina. Then, she was invited by the Admin to the Faculty room and there Dr. Ponsica informed everybody that she was the new teacher to take the place of Charina.
